At-Home Workouts

You don’t need fancy gym equipment to get in shape—many effective exercises can be done with just your body weight or everyday items you already have at home. Bodyweight exercises, such as squats, push-ups, planks, lunges, and glute bridges, are great starting points.

Exercises:

  • Squats help strengthen your legs, glutes, and core.
  • Push-ups target the chest, arms, and core.
  • Planks are excellent for building core strength.
  • Lunges engage your legs and glutes.
  • Glute bridges activate the posterior chain.

Replace Weights:

  • If you don’t have dumbbells or kettlebells you can use house items.
  • For a makeshift weight, fill a backpack with books or other objects.
  • Towels can be used for resistance exercises or you can get resistance bands for cheap.
